## The Missed Call Problem
For many local service businesses, dental clinics, and restaurants in Calgary, the phone is the primary lifeline for new revenue. Yet, statistics show that nearly **40% of all small business calls go to voicemail or ring out**, especially during peak hours, lunch breaks, or outside of standard business hours. 

The harsh reality? In 2026, a potential customer sent to voicemail rarely leaves a message. They hang up and call the next business on the Google search results page. Every missed call is a missed opportunity — and often, a lead handed directly to a competitor.
